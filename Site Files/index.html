Daniel Kogler

Daniel Kogler is a husband and a father in Baton Rouge, Louisiana, who loves writing fantasy, adventure, and sci-fi. He is a Ph.D. graduate with a concentration in high performance computing, and he is currently working as an instructor teaching kids, young adults, and high school teachers programming skills. Aside from writing, Daniel enjoys taking his son to parks, immersing himself in fictional worlds, and exploring beautiful scenery in real life both above and below water.
