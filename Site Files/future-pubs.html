The Witch of World's End: The Book of Prophecy

The first book of The Witch of World's End series follows a prideful young witch named Lyn after she learns of the titular Book of Prophecies and the royal order of heroes training to rid the kingdom of witchcraft. Though little is known of the contents of the book, it is clear that it's by the book's direction that the crown has redoubled its efforts to snuff out dark magic.

Accompanied by her adoptive sister Zahra and a mysterious thief named Faux, Lyn must find a way to infiltrate the order of heroes and claim the bothersome book for herself. It could just prove to be the edge witches need to finally fight back against the royal family. Then again, the secrets within might just be enough to make Lyn question her own hopes and ambitions.

The Armonia Series

The Armonia series will primarily follow Lapis Kaghani in her journey across the world in a quest to bring about a cure for the Blight. For millennia, this supernatural plague has slowly but unyieldingly spread across the land, corrupting mind, body, and even matter itself.

To succeed in her journey, Lapis will need to overcome longstanding animosity between the eight races of the world. Little will she realize that her journey serves another purpose, as a plan decades in the making unfolds around her.
